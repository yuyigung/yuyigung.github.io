## Research Themes

### 1. Planetary Seismology
Using seismic observations to investigate the internal structure of planetary bodies.

### 2. Mantle Structure and Dynamics
Imaging the Earth's mantle using seismic and electromagnetic methods.

### 3. Integrated Geophysics
Combining seismology with magnetotellurics and numerical modeling.
